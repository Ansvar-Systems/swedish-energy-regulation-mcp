## Currency and Staleness

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by weeks or months
- Swedish energy regulation changes through new lagar, forordningar, foreskrifter, and regulatory decisions
- A regulation marked "in force" may have been amended or repealed since the last database update
- This Tool does NOT automatically track amendments

## Coverage Gaps

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Full legal text** -- contains representative summaries only
2. **Court decisions** -- Forvaltningsratten and Kammarratten energy rulings are not covered
3. **EU energy directives** -- covered separately by the EU Regulations MCP
4. **Political energy agreements** -- Energioverenskommelsen is not included
5. **Municipal energy plans** -- local authority publications are not covered
6. **Historical regulation versions** -- limited to current in-force versions
